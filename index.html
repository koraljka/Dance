Welcome to my Balance project. Idea of this project is to show how different types of media can be put together to form a balance using light design, photography, video and web technologies. Photos were taken using smoke, dust, bubbles and different types of light to form a balance between all of those elements. The screen is split into two identical sized images and even though images are not conventionally symmetrical, all of them are put together to form a balance in a way of colours used, entropy of each image and size.
